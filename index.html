Welcome to the Wellness Predict

Use this application to predict the likelihood of diabetes, heart disease, or Parkinson's disease based on your input data. You can also find some healthy lifestyle tips to improve your overall well-being.

Get your reports done

Go to laboratory and ensure you have all necessary medical reports prepared for analysis.

Enter all the details from the report

Input all relevant information accurately from your medical reports into the system.

Click on predict results

Once all details are entered, proceed to predict the potential outcomes.

Your results will be shown

After prediction, view your potential results to gain insights into your health condition.

Warnings Before Use

Don't totally rely on results as accuracy is around 75%.
Consult Doctors before taking any medicines and any treatment.
Suitable for the use for doctors or persons with medical knowledge.
